AI Boom Sends Hardware Prices Through the Roof

If you've been shoping for new memory, SSDs or a GPU lately, you've probably noticed prices climing quickly. THe massive boom in AI system building is consuming hardware stock at an unprecedented rate.

Why Everything Costs More

AI training rigs and data center clusters are absorbing huge amounts of DRAM, NVMe storage, and high-end GPUs. Demand hasn't been this intense since the crypto-mining era, and manufactures are struggling tokeep up.

Memory

DDR4 price are rising as manufactures shift production to high-bandwidth server memory for AI workloads. Reduced supply for consumers leads to higher costs.

SSDs

AI models require masswive volumes of fast storage. Enterprise NVME drive are being purchased first, which is pushing up consumer SSD prices.

GPUs

High-end accelerators are sold out for months. Even gaming GPUs are being bought by developers,labs, and hobbyists.

Impact on System Builders

Server builders or CPU builders are experiencing longer leads times,reduced availibility,and increased component costs-especially for GPU-accelerated or high-memory systems. Prices are expected to stay eleved until production scales up.
